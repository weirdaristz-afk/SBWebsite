📚 The Lore of Sweeties Boutique 📚

Aurora, The Star Guardian 🌟

Born from a fallen star ✨ that landed in a field of cosmic dust, Aurora is the gentle guardian of the Cosmic Heart 💖. It is her sworn duty to protect the balance of magic throughout the galaxy and ensure that the power of creativity and friendship never fades.

She uses her celestial powers to weave starlight into beautiful accessories, which she shares with those she deems worthy. Her lucky mirror 🪞 can open pathways to any place she has visited, allowing her and her friends to travel in the blink of an eye.

Clover, The Lucky Companion 🍀

Not just any unicorn 🦄, Clover was found at the end of a rainbow 🌈 that touched a four-leaf clover field on a forgotten asteroid. As a result, he is bursting with good luck and fortune, which he loves to share!

He can sniff out hidden clovers and rare materials across the galaxy, making him the perfect companion for any aspiring boutique owner. His cheerful attitude and unwavering loyalty make him Aurora's most trusted friend.
